SALZBURG MEDIA ART AWARD

Since 2006 annually, the country of Salzburg, in cooperation with Schmiede Hallein, supports a media art project from Salzburg with a 10.000Eur grant. The grant is intended to enable the realization of the proposed project. Project proposals should not be longer than five pages. Applicants need to be born in, currently be studying or officially be living in Salzburg. Project proposal deadline is May 15th. The winning project/team will participate at the current Schmiede for development and networking and will be presented on the second Friday of next Schmiede.
